About Us

“Shadow Play” is a platform where gamers and streamers will find everything they need to stay on the verge! News, tournaments, community, and even merch creation - all in one place.

Gamers of all ranges can select their favorite tournament, build teams, find the best streams, and never miss what is happening in their favorite universe!

Streamers can organize international tournaments, invite their supporters to communities, and rapidly expand their audience!

Merch for everyone: share your idea and estimated budget, and let us do the rest! The “Shadow Play” team will develop a full design pack and handle all operations from printing to shipping!
